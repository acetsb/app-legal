Privacy Policy — 1Fintech_Poster

Last updated: 23 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how 1Fintech_Poster ("the App") handles information in connection with its use of the TikTok API.

Data Collected

The App accesses TikTok account credentials and OAuth tokens solely for the purpose of publishing video content on behalf of authorised accounts. No personal data is collected from third parties or end users.

How Data Is Used

OAuth tokens are stored securely on a private server and are used exclusively to authenticate API requests to TikTok. Tokens are never shared with or sold to any third party.

Data Retention

Access tokens are retained only as long as necessary to perform content posting functions and are deleted upon account disconnection.

Third Parties

The App does not share any data with third parties. It communicates solely with the TikTok API as part of its core functionality.
